Membership

Ownership opens August 14, 2026.

RegenHub, LCA is a cooperative — its members are its owners. There are four membership classes, each designed for a different relationship to the cooperative's work.

Class One

Community Supporter

The entry point. Community supporters are connected to the cooperative's work and community. A way to be part of what is being built without carrying the obligations of governance or labor.

No voting rights. Open to anyone who supports the cooperative's purpose.
Class Two

Hub Member

Members who participate in the RegenHub community and spaces. Patron members with governance rights — a vote in the cooperative's decisions and a stake in its direction.

Patron member with governance rights. Participation in the cooperative's community and spaces.
Class Three

Labor Member

Members who contribute labor to the cooperative's work. The core working class. Patronage is allocated in proportion to labor contributed — the more you build, the more you earn.

Full patron member rights. Patronage allocated by labor contributed to the cooperative.
Class Four

Investor

Capital members who provide investment to the cooperative. Class Four rights are defined by agreement — structured to align capital with the cooperative's long-term purpose rather than short-term return.

Capital member. Rights and terms defined by individual agreement.

Membership opens August 14, 2026. To be notified, watch this space.

Read the Membership Agreement and Bylaws at /legal/ for the full legal terms of each membership class.
