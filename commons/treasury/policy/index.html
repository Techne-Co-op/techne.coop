§1

The floor

No disbursement of the cooperative's funds on fewer than two distinct human acts: one proposal and at least one approval, and the proposer and any approver are never the same person. Declining is as recorded as approving; a refusal names its grounds. The system prepares completely and a person executes at the bank; no instrument, agent, or automation holds a key or initiates a movement (TREASURY §7, Law X).

§4

Standing purposes

Pre-authorized purposes, inside band 1 limits: a dues refund; a documented expense reimbursement against a receipt; a tax-covering distribution recorded under an adopted Allocation Run (PATRONAGE §11), which discharges a commitment already governed by its own sequence. Everything not on this list escalates to its band by amount, and anything novel in kind escalates to the board regardless of amount.

§5

The principal Programs

The cooperative's activity organizes under Programs, the organizing bodies of PATRONAGE §4, and two stand as principal. RegenHub.xyz is the workspace operations Program: the cooperatively run workspace at 1515 Walnut, its coworking rhythms, day passes, and event operations, the activity the Articles name first. techne.coop is the craft guild Program: the network of self-organizing communities of practice, its courses, gatherings, and network services. Other Programs follow by the same acts; these two are named so the FSC sizes the bands and the standing purposes with the real flows in view.

Three provisions draw the program joint through this policy. First, a money.received whose purpose is a program payment carries the Program agent in its payload, and its book partition follows Law IX: member capital facts ride the entity book, grant-bounded program flows ride the host book, exactly as PATRONAGE pinned it. Second, funding bounds are not this instrument's to set: each Program's POLICY-PATRONAGE instrument carries its own bounds (PATRONAGE §7), and a disbursement drawing on a Program's bound adds that Program's Coordinator of record to its approver trail, a proposed provision the FSC may strike. Third, naming designates nothing: a Program stands when its agent enters by recorded act and binds when its policy adopts by board act; this section names intent, not standing.
