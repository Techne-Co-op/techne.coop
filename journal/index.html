Journal

The Techne journal is a shared space for common work and daybook entries — tools for collective thinking, developed in the open.

Writing here is not broadcast; it is correspondence. Notes on what is being built, what was learned, what is still unresolved. The journal belongs to the cooperative and to anyone who reads it carefully.
